Developers and businesses need to Build, Run, and Scale apps, everywhere, in minutes</h2><p>If you are building a modern application, it’s going to have APIs, workers, and databases that need to be deployed somewhere in the cloud or at the edge. But how can you do that in minutes with a state-of-the-art infrastructure? Why is it still so complicated for teams to build, run, and scale applications?</p><p>Here is what we see: backend developers struggle to deploy their apps and months of work are wasted in companies of all sizes to build efficient infrastructure. As a software engineer deploying to production, you have to pick between:</p><ol>
<li><strong>Complexity</strong>: somehow the standard solution is being an expert with all cloud technologies of the market starting with <a href="https://www.koyeb.com/blog/the-true-cost-of-kubernetes-people-time-and-productivity">Kubernetes and spending months building your own stack</a>. If you ask us, this doesn’t make much sense.</li>
<li><strong>Simple solutions lacking flexibility:</strong> the other end of the spectrum is simple platforms with limited features that don’t properly scale as your business grows.</li>
</ol><p>When deploying applications, there are a few things that are essential and shouldn’t be traded:</p><ul>
<li><strong>No cloud provider-specific</strong> <strong>skills</strong>: you shouldn’t have to rewrite your app or get an AWS certification to go live. Going from local to global should be easy with standard and known technologies. No need to learn countless technologies to deploy a state-of-the-art app.</li>
<li><strong>Built-in continuous deployment</strong>: a simple git repository or docker container should be enough and the platform should provide the build primitives. It should be fast to deploy to production to be able to quickly iterate.</li>
<li><strong>No infrastructure management</strong>: the experience should be Serverless. No thoughts about hardware or orchestration.</li>
<li><strong>Native resiliency and scalability</strong>: abstracting infrastructure management means resiliency and ability to scale should be built-in.</li>
<li><strong>High performances globally</strong>: you should be able to deploy anywhere and everywhere with high-performance servers able to support demanding workloads.</li>
<li><strong>Sustainable costs</strong>: no cloud architect needed or crazy bill to have high-level features with decent performance, this should be the norm.</li>
</ul><p>In the world of cloud infrastructure, Serverless is the new standard experience. First-generation serverless platforms offer resiliency and scalability, yet necessitate trade-offs in terms of simplicity, portability, performance, and costs. No one needs to make or accept these tradeoffs. We’re building what the serverless experience should be.</p><h2 id="why-us-serverless-deployments-backed-by-foundational-technology">Why us?
